How It Works

★ Photobooth Guide ★
1

Pick your look

Choose a filter (6 vintage styles) and a frame color (black or white). Your choices lock in before capture.

2

Hit the big red button

The booth captures 4 photos in a row. You'll get a 3-second countdown before each one — strike a different pose!

3

Watch the flash

A bright flash fires when each photo is taken. Hold still for the moment of capture.

4

Wait for it to develop

Your photos go through the vintage filter pipeline. Takes a couple seconds.

5

Save your strip

Download as a PNG, retake if you don't love it, or start a new session.

Leave the booth?

Your session won't be saved.
